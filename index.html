I'm currently a Ph.D. student in Department of Mathematics at Texas A&M University, working under the supervision of Dr. Matthew Papanikolas.

I obtained my bachelor's degree in Department of Mathematics at Southern University of Science and Technology (南方科技大学) in July 2020.

I'm interested in number theory and arithmetic geometry(数论与算术几何), especially in Elliptic curves, Abelian varieties and their function field analogues called Drinfeld modules and A-motives. Currently, I'm mainly interested in A-motives, shtukas, Drinfeld modules and L-series attached to these arithmetic objects. I'm also interested in topics like Drinfeld modular forms, Drinfeld modular varieties and motivic interpretation of special values and special functions.
